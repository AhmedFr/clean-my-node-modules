How it works

Three steps to a lighter Mac.

01

Get it & it scans

Buy the signed .app — or clone the repo and build your own. The first scan maps every node_modules folder on your disk.

$pnpm install && pnpm package
02

Set your limit

Pick a threshold in gigabytes and how often to rescan — every 6 hours, daily, or weekly. That's the entire setup.

limit 5 GB · scan daily
03

Clean in a click

When you cross the line, review the stale folders and send them to the Trash. Your disk thanks you.
